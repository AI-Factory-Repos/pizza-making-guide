Visual Doneness Indicators

Use these visual cues to know exactly when your pizza is perfectly baked.

Golden Brown Edge
Crust Color

🟤 Crust Color

  • Pale / White — needs more time
  • Golden Brown — perfectly done
  • Dark Brown / Black — overdone

The bottom of the crust should be an even golden brown when lifted with a spatula.

Bubbling & Melted
Cheese

🧀 Cheese Bubbling

  • Solid / Unmelted — not ready
  • Bubbling with light brown spots — perfect
  • Fully charred / separated — too long

Look for active bubbling across most of the surface with some golden-brown spots on the peaks.

Browned Edges
Edge Browning

🔥 Edge Browning

  • Doughy & pale — needs more time
  • Puffed & golden with char spots — perfect
  • Completely charred — overdone

Edges should puff up and show leopard-spot char marks for authentic Neapolitan style.
